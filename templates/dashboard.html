Questions & Answers

A few helpful notes for the wedding weekend. If you still have questions, please reach out to us directly.

Guest Notice

What should I wear?

Please refer to the attire notes listed under each event in the Schedule tab. Bright festive colors are perfect for Haldi, traditional Indian formal attire is encouraged for the Hindu ceremony, and formal or semi-formal attire is welcome for the Christian wedding and reception.

Invitation

Can I bring a guest?

Please refer to your invitation and RSVP options in the Schedule tab. If your invitation includes additional guests, you will be able to enter that number while submitting your RSVP.

Family

Are children invited?

Please refer to your invitation details. If children are included in your invitation, they can be counted in your RSVP guest count.

Arrival

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 20–30 minutes before the listed event start time so you have enough time for parking, seating, and settling in.

Parking

Will parking be available?

Parking details will depend on the event location. Please use the directions link under each event, and we will share any specific parking instructions closer to the wedding weekend.

Food

Will food be provided?

Yes, food will be provided at the wedding events. If you have dietary restrictions or allergies, please include them in the notes section when submitting your RSVP.

Photos

Can I take photos?

We would love for you to capture memories during the celebrations. During formal ceremony moments, please be mindful of the photographer and the couple’s view.

Travel

Where should I stay?

Please check the Travel tab for airport information, recommended stay areas, things to do, and restaurant suggestions. Hotel block details will be shared there when available.

Help Desk

Who should I contact?

If you have questions about your invitation, RSVP, travel, or wedding details, please reach out to Adlin or Prithvi directly.
