Questions & Answers

A few helpful notes for the wedding weekend. Your private invitation and the Schedule tab remain the best source for event-specific details.

Attire What should I wear?

Please refer to the attire details under each celebration in the Schedule tab. Festive yellow shades are encouraged for Haldi, soft pastel and floral shades for the Telugu Wedding, and green shades for the Christian Wedding and Reception.

Invitation Can I bring a guest?

Your RSVP form shows the exact number of guests included with your invitation. That limit is unique to your private invitation link.

Family Are children invited?

Please follow the guest count shown with your invitation. Children included in your invitation can be counted when you submit the RSVP.

Arrival What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 20–30 minutes before the listed start time for parking, seating, and a relaxed arrival before the celebration begins.

Parking Will parking be available?

Parking varies by venue. Use the Directions button under each event, and check back closer to the wedding weekend for any special parking instructions.

Food Will food be provided?

Yes. Please add dietary restrictions, allergies, or accessibility needs in the Notes field when submitting your RSVP for the relevant event.

Photos Can I take photos?

We would love for you to capture memories. During formal ceremony moments, please remain mindful of the professional photographer and the couple’s view.

Travel Where should I stay?

The Travel tab includes airports, recommended stay areas, local activities, and hotel information. The hotel booking link will be added when finalized.
